About us

The London Web is a regular meetup that takes place on the third Thursday of every month at the Radisson Blu Kenilworth. The event meetup group is run by Future Insights, a small, international events company based in Bath (UK) and Boston (USA). Our mission is to serve the designers, developers, entrepreneurs and everyone else making the web a better place. We produce high quality conferences, articles, videos and newsletters for the people who make the internet awesome and applications shine. We're always learning and always discovering.

Visit us at futureinsights.com.
